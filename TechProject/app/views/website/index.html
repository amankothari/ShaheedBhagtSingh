Why SHAHEED BHAGAT SINGH COLLEGE ?

Ichhawar a renowned city, still it is educationally weak due to scholastic reasons. It is deprived of technologies, professional syllabus and employment techniques, due to which, students of Ichhawar have pressure to take up education in different cities . So with one aim to help students, “ Diwadiya Shiksha Samiti “ has established ‘ SHAHEED BHAGAT SINGH COLLEGE‘ on the name of great freedom fighter ‘SHAHEED BHAGAT SINGH’.
