Welcome to SHAHEED BHAGAT SINGH COLLEGE

Ichhawar a renowned city, still it is educationally weak due to scholastic reasons. It is deprived of technologies, professional syllabus and employment techniques, due to which, students of Ichhawar have pressure to take up education in different cities . So with one aim to help students, “ Diwadiya Shiksha Samiti “ has established ‘ SHAHEED BHAGAT SINGH COLLEGE‘ on the name of great freedom fighter ‘SHAHEED BHAGAT SINGH’.

  • Our organization setup in 2001
  • Improve that level of education around Ichhawar area
  • free education for financially week student
  • job oriented courses running
  • provide better opportunity to student in sports and cultural activity
  • improve technical education
  • better environment for better education
  • free Wi-Fi campuses around college
  • all teacher in code 28 for batter education
  • separate department for student

So with one aim to help students, “ Diwadiya Shiksha Samiti “ has established ‘ SHAHEED BHAGAT SINGH COLLEGE‘ on the name of great freedom fighter ‘SHAHEED BHAGAT SINGH’.

Mr. Amit Verma
Principle, College Green Online

Samiti has taken initiative to promote best education which enables students of rural and urban area of Ichhawar to compete with other student of different cities in employment and business sector. College is fully equipped with techniques and technologies which will be fruitful for their further career. Our organization also helps the students financially by providing scholarship according to the MP GOVT rule.

This is our 3rd year of establishment and we are fortunate to have satisfactory results each year. Graduated students were satisfied with our teachings and we put our continuous efforts for the betterment of college. College will be it’s own building from Next sesion and our college has always been supportive for SC,ST and OBC students too, To provide benefits of Government plans.

Our Mission

Colleges around the world had played very important role in transforming the society. Shaheed Bhagat Singh College is one of the leading College of the District. It has strong foundation for creating a powerful academic future. The priority is to create a vivid, diversified academic and research landscape to the district and nation, to the aspirants and to the overall society.

Innovation driven education system is the core value of Shaheed Bhagat Singh College. We are committed to strive the perfect balance of taking the legacy of our College and the modern College culture. It is our aim to ensure that the Shaheed Bhagat Singh College should be considered as one of the leading College not only at district level but at state levels. We are looking forward to attract leading academician and researcher and to provide them with conducive environment, to assist them in providing quality education to the aspirants. The college envisages to create advance laboratories in various disciplines like science and technology, commerce, etc.
